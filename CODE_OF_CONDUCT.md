# Code of Conduct

FlagForge contributors are expected to maintain a respectful, constructive, and inclusive environment.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code without personal attacks.
- Give specific, actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent while remaining open to correction.
- Respect privacy and never publish credentials or private user data.
- Prioritize a safe learning environment for contributors at different experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption, or publication of private information is not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior violates these expectations. Reports should be sent privately to the repository owner rather than posted publicly.
